Founder of UTC

Biography of Mr. Than Naing Win

Mr. Than Naing Win, born on May 3, 1985, in Mandalay, Myanmar, is an accomplished IT professional, educator, and entrepreneur. He has lived his entire life in Mandalay, contributing significantly to the local technology and education sectors.

Education

Mr. Than Naing Win began his formal education at Basic Education High School (B.E.H.S.) No. 11, where he studied until Grade 9. He completed his Grade 10 education at B.E.H.S. No. 7. His academic journey continued with his enrollment at the University of Computer Studies, Mandalay (UCSM), where he earned a Bachelor of Computer Technology (B.C.Tech) degree in 2005. Recognizing the importance of communication skills in the global era, he pursued a Postgraduate Diploma in English (P.G.D.E) at the University of Foreign Languages, Mandalay, from 2005 to 2007, successfully earning his diploma in 2008.

Professional Career

Mr. Than Naing Win's professional career is marked by a blend of entrepreneurial ventures, technical expertise, and academic contributions.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

In 2009, he founded Win Computer, a Computer Services and Training Center, which he successfully managed until 2014. This venture allowed him to impart IT skills and provide technical support to the local community. In 2021, he established Universal Technology IT Solution in Mandalay, where he serves as the CEO. This company offers a wide range of IT solutions and continues to play a pivotal role in advancing technology in the region.

Corporate Experience

From 2016 to 2021, Mr. Than Naing Win worked at Mo Mo International Trading Co., Ltd. as an IT Technician and Digital Marketing Supervisor. During this time, he honed his technical skills and gained valuable experience in digital marketing strategies.

Academic Contributions

Mr. Than Naing Win has a long-standing passion for education. He began teaching as a Senior Lecturer at iNet College in 2017, a role he continues to this day. His dedication to education expanded further when he joined Host Myanmar Institute in 2022, where he also serves as a lecturer. Through his teaching roles, he has inspired countless students to pursue careers in IT and digital technologies.

Leadership and Vision

As the CEO of Universal Technology IT Solution, Mr. Than Naing Win is committed to providing cutting-edge IT solutions and fostering innovation in the technology sector. His leadership is driven by a vision to empower individuals and businesses with the tools and knowledge needed to succeed in the digital age.

Personal Life

Mr. Than Naing Win continues to live in Mandalay, where he balances his professional endeavors with a dedication to his community. He is deeply passionate about education, technology, and the transformative power of digital solutions.

Conclusion

With his extensive experience in IT, education, and business, Mr. Than Naing Win has become a respected figure in Mandalay’s technology and academic sectors. His journey is a testament to his commitment to lifelong learning, innovation, and service to his community.
